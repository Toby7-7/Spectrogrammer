Spectrogrammer is a real-time audio spectrum analyzer designed primarily for Android phones, while still keeping a Linux build path for local development.

Main features:
- Real-time spectrum trace and waterfall
- Linear / logarithmic / music-log / Mel / Bark / ERB frequency scales
- Peak-hold trace, peak markers, and configurable falloff
- Manual cursor line with live frequency and dB readout
- Pinch zoom and horizontal pan for local frequency inspection
- Adjustable audio source, sample rate, FFT size, decimation, window function, scroll speed, and smoothing
- Stereo modes including mono, left, right, stereo mix, stereo difference, and stereo split
- English by default, with an in-app switch to Simplified Chinese
- Background capture and keep-screen-on support

Notes:
- High sample rates depend on device and driver support.
- The APK is mainly intended for local installation and debugging.
